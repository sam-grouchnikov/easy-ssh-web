Easy-SSH

Easy-SSH is your one stop shop for AI model training, intended for PyTorch + W&B-based workflows. This integrated development environment allows you to manage all aspects of your projects in one place, with every featured tailored towards training AI models on remote GPUs via SSH. See below for licensing and transparency.

Capabilities

Command Line

Access your remote GPU clusters and run scripts on them with a simple, Gemini-inspired chat interface. Use button-based navigation and actions for easy, annoyance free actions.

File Editor

View all your remote files, edit them, and transfer them to your local machine with minimal effort.

Log Viewer

View your Weights & Biases logs in-app, with live-updates (user-activated) for quick, convenient model training progress monitoring.

Licensing and Transparency
